Beef Taco

Here is a recipe for classic, flavorful ground beef tacos that you can make in less than 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80-90% lean)
  • ½ cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 tbsp oil (optional, if needed for lean beef)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp salt (to taste)
  • ¼ tsp black pepper (to taste)
  • 8-12 taco shells or soft tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook the beef
  2. Season the meat
  3. Simmer
  4. Prepare Tortillas
  5. Assemble and serve

Bon Appetit!
